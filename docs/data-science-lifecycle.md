# Data Science Lifecycle

## Question -> Data -> Insight

The core workflow for this project is simple:

1. Question: What problems are citizens reporting, where do they happen, and how quickly are they resolved?
2. Data: Collect grievance records with timestamps, categories, descriptions, locations, and resolution status.
3. Insight: Summarize trends, detect hotspots, and communicate patterns that can guide action.

## How it applies here

- The project question focuses on municipal service quality.
- The data includes complaint metadata and text descriptions.
- The insight layer highlights recurring issues, slow responses, and high-volume areas.

## Why this matters

The lifecycle keeps analysis practical. Instead of exploring data without direction, each step is tied to a decision-making goal for municipal stakeholders.
